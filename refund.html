Refund Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2025

1. 30-Day Satisfaction Guarantee

We stand behind our service quality. If you're not completely satisfied with your Xtreamlinc service within the first 30 days, we offer a full refund of service charges paid.

  • Refund applies to monthly service charges only
  • Installation fees and equipment charges are non-refundable
  • Refund must be requested within 30 days of service activation
  • Service must be cancelled to receive refund

2. Refund Eligibility

To be eligible for a refund, customers must:

  • Be within the 30-day satisfaction guarantee period
  • Have attempted to resolve service issues through customer support
  • Return all equipment in good working condition
  • Have no outstanding balances on their account
  • Provide a valid reason for service dissatisfaction

3. Non-Refundable Items

The following charges are non-refundable under all circumstances:

  • Installation Fees: Professional installation and setup charges
  • Equipment Fees: Purchase price of customer-owned equipment
  • Activation Fees: One-time account setup and activation charges
  • Late Fees: Penalties for overdue payments
  • Service Calls: Charges for service visits and repairs
  • Third-Party Services: Premium channels or add-on services

4. Refund Process

To request a refund, follow these steps:

  1. Contact Customer Service: Call (888) 928-3252 to initiate the refund process
  2. Provide Information: Share your account details and reason for refund request
  3. Schedule Equipment Return: Arrange for equipment pickup or return
  4. Account Review: We'll review your account and verify eligibility
  5. Processing: Approved refunds are processed within 5-10 business days

5. Prorated Refunds

In certain situations, prorated refunds may be available:

  • Service Outages: Credits for qualifying extended outages
  • Mid-Cycle Cancellation: Refunds for unused service periods (subject to contract terms)
  • Plan Changes: Adjustments when downgrading service plans
  • Billing Errors: Corrections for incorrect charges

6. Equipment Return Requirements

All Xtreamlinc equipment must be returned in good condition:

  • Equipment must be returned within 30 days of service termination
  • Original packaging is preferred but not required
  • Equipment must be in working order with minimal wear
  • Damaged or unreturned equipment will be charged to your account
  • We provide prepaid shipping labels for equipment returns

7. Promotional Offers and Contracts

Special considerations apply to promotional offers:

  • Customers under promotional contracts may be subject to early termination fees
  • Promotional pricing refunds are calculated at the regular service rate
  • Gift cards and promotional credits are non-refundable
  • Contract terms supersede standard refund policies where applicable

8. Refund Method and Timeline

Approved refunds are processed as follows:

  • Credit Card: Refunds to original payment method within 5-10 business days
  • Bank Transfer: Direct deposit to checking account within 7-14 business days
  • Check: Mailed refund check within 10-15 business days
  • Account Credit: Applied to account for future billing cycles

9. Dispute Resolution

If you're unsatisfied with our refund decision, you may:

  • Request escalation to a supervisor or manager
  • File a formal complaint through our customer portal
  • Contact regulatory authorities in your jurisdiction
  • Pursue binding arbitration as outlined in our Terms & Conditions

10. Policy Changes

This refund policy may be updated periodically. Changes will be communicated through:

  • Email notifications to active customers
  • Updates posted on our website
  • Account notifications in customer portal
  • Billing statement inserts where applicable

11. Contact Information

For refund requests or questions about this policy:

  • Phone: (888) 928-3252
  • Email: billing@xtreamlinc.com
  • Address: Xtreamlinc Billing Department, 123 Tech Boulevard, Digital City, DC 12345
  • Hours: 24/7 Customer Support
