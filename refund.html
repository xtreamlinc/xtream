Refund Policy

Fair and transparent refund terms. We stand behind our service quality.

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

1. Money-Back Guarantee

30-Day Satisfaction Guarantee: If you're not completely satisfied with your Xtreamlinc service within the first 30 days, we'll provide a full refund of monthly service charges.

What's Included

  • Full refund of monthly service fees
  • Pro-rated refund for partial month usage
  • No questions asked within guarantee period
  • Free equipment pickup and return

What's Not Included

  • Installation fees for custom work
  • Third-party service charges
  • Equipment damage or loss fees
  • Services used beyond 30-day period

2. Refund Eligibility

To qualify for a refund under our policy:

  • Request must be made within 30 days of service activation
  • Account must be in good standing (no unpaid balances)
  • All Xtreamlinc equipment must be returned in working condition
  • Service cancellation must be completed through official channels

Special Circumstances

  • Service outages exceeding 72 hours may qualify for service credits
  • Technical issues preventing normal use may extend guarantee period
  • Billing errors will be corrected with appropriate credits
  • Disputed charges reviewed case-by-case

3. Refund Process

We've streamlined our refund process for your convenience:

How to Request

  • Call our customer service at (888) 928-3252
  • Email refund@xtreamlinc.com with account details
  • Visit our online customer portal
  • Speak with any customer service representative

Processing Timeline

  • Refund approval within 2 business days
  • Equipment return scheduled within 5 business days
  • Refund processed within 5-7 business days after equipment return
  • Credits appear on original payment method

4. Equipment Return

All Xtreamlinc equipment must be returned for refund completion:

Equipment List

  • Modem and router devices
  • Cable boxes and remote controls
  • Power adapters and cables
  • Any additional accessories provided

Return Process

  • FREE equipment pickup scheduled at your convenience
  • Professional technician handles disconnection
  • Return receipt provided for your records
  • Equipment condition assessed upon return

5. Service Credits

For service issues that don't require full cancellation:

Automatic Credits

  • Service outages over 24 hours
  • Significant speed reductions below 50% of plan
  • Billing system errors
  • Missed installation appointments

Credit Amounts

  • Outages: Daily pro-rated service credit
  • Speed issues: Partial monthly credit
  • Installation delays: $50 service credit
  • Billing errors: Full correction plus inconvenience credit

6. Special Promotions

Promotional offers may have specific refund terms:

  • Promotional pricing honored through guarantee period
  • Gift cards and promotional credits are non-refundable
  • Bundle discounts refunded proportionally
  • Referral bonuses subject to continued service

7. Business Accounts

Business customers have enhanced refund protections:

  • Extended 60-day evaluation period
  • Service level agreement with uptime guarantees
  • Priority support and issue resolution
  • Custom refund terms available for enterprise accounts

8. Dispute Resolution

If you're not satisfied with our refund decision:

  • Escalate to supervisor or manager level
  • Request review by customer advocacy team
  • Submit formal complaint for executive review
  • Alternative dispute resolution options available

9. Contact for Refunds

Multiple ways to request refunds or ask questions:

Phone: (888) 928-3252
Email: refund@xtreamlinc.com
Live Chat: Available 24/7 on our website
Customer Advocate: advocate@xtreamlinc.com

Our Promise: We're committed to making things right. If you're experiencing issues with our service, let us work with you to find a solution before considering cancellation.
